Purpose: Central router for all service access

Key Functions:
  - getService(name, options) - Get service instance
  - getMode() - Check current mode (mock/production)

                      SUPPORT & TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Issue: Services not loading
Solution: export MOCK_MODE=true

Issue: Storage permission error
Solution: mkdir -p tmp/mock_storage/{emails,documents,inbox}

Issue: Slow streaming
Solution: export MOCK_ANTHROPIC_DELAY_MS=100

Issue: Storage not clearing
Solution: rm -rf tmp/mock_storage

Issue: Service already cached
Solution: Call clearCache() from service factory
